Will be declined if:
1. Non-Libre license or our unsuitable licenses (See FreePats)
2. Lower than 44.1 khz 16bit samples (FP standard)
3. Processed via commercial / non-libre tools (e.g. Guitar Rig); Electric Exclusive
4. Sampled from 3rd Party Materials (like Arranger Keyboards), commercial, non-libre and unknown sources
5. Non-Libre Synthesizers

Synthesizer / Tools that I use:

Dexed: DX7-esqued / FM Synthes
Surge, ZynAddSubFX: Main Synthes
OB-Xd: Oberheim-esqued Tones
Juno 6: JUNO-esqued Tones
setBfree: Drawbar Organs
